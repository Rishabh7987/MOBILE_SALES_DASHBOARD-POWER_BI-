# MOBILE_SALES_DASHBOARD-POWER_BI-

📊 Mobile Sales Dashboard – Analytical Report
🧾 Executive Summary

The dashboard highlights strong mobile sales performance with hundreds of millions in revenue, consistent monthly demand, and balanced payment preferences. While overall growth is steady, there are clear opportunities in regional expansion, product optimization, and customer satisfaction improvement.

💰 Overall Performance Insights
Total Sales: ~769M (overall view), ~253M (filtered), ~19M (MTD)
Total Quantity Sold: ~19K units
Total Transactions: ~4K
Average Price: ~40K

👉 Insight:
The relatively high average price (~40K) suggests a focus on mid-to-premium smartphone segments, not budget devices.

📈 Sales Trend Analysis
Monthly Trends
Peak months: March (~1696 units) and July (~1700 units)
Dip observed in February (~1451 units) and September (~1521 units)

👉 Insight:

Sales show seasonal fluctuations
Likely driven by:
Product launches
Festive/discount seasons
Market demand cycles
MTD (Month-To-Date) Growth
Strong upward trajectory from ~0.6M to ~18.9M
No major declines → consistent daily sales growth

👉 Insight:

Indicates healthy ongoing demand
Sales momentum is stable without volatility

🔍 Key Business Insights
Strengths

✅ Strong overall revenue
✅ Consistent MTD growth
✅ Premium product dominance
✅ Balanced payment ecosystem

Weaknesses

⚠️ Slight YoY decline
⚠️ Limited reach beyond metro cities
⚠️ Some customer dissatisfaction
⚠️ Sales dips in specific months/days

🚀 Recommendations
1. Boost Growth
Launch campaigns during low-performing months (Feb, Sept)
Introduce seasonal offers
2. Improve Market Reach
Expand into Tier-2 & Tier-3 cities
Strengthen distribution channels
3. Optimize Product Strategy
Focus on high-performing models
Reduce low-performing inventory
4. Enhance Customer Experience
Improve after-sales service
Address “Poor” ratings quickly
5. Increase Sales Consistency
Introduce weekday promotions
Target low-sales days (Thursday)
6. Reverse YoY Decline
Competitive pricing strategies
New product launches
Stronger marketing campaigns
📌 Conclusion

The business is performing well with strong revenue and steady demand, but faces challenges in growth consistency and market expansion. By focusing on customer satisfaction, regional growth, and strategic promotions, the company can significantly improve its performance and regain positive year-over-year growth.
